What is predict?

Predict is an online dashboard that helps patients and clinicians to predict the likelihood
of developing Dengue Hemorrhagic Fever (DHF) using Cytokine parameters.This will help
the clinicians to institute appropriate treatment before the patient enters the critical phase.

Predict is for clinicians, patients and their families.
